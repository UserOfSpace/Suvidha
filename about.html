About Us

Welcome to Suvidha Share Foundation

Suvidha Share Foundation, established in 1995, is a non-profit organization dedicated to serving the poor and hungry. For nearly three decades, we have worked with compassion and commitment to provide nutritious meals to those in need. Our mission is to ensure that no one goes to bed hungry, and we strive every day to make that vision a reality. Through our food distribution programs, we reach vulnerable communities across slums, streets, and shelters. With the generous support of our donors, we have been able to impact thousands of lives. At Suvidha, we believe that small acts of kindness can lead to big change. You can also be a part of this journey by donating food to help those who need it most. Together, we can build a more caring, hunger-free society.
